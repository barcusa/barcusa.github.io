We've been across the pond and around the block

Ever since we were the new bank in London back in 1690, Barclays has been bringing people closer to their financial goals with industry-leading innovations. Through it all, we’ve made it our business to put our customers first and move them forward. It’s that spirit that continues to inspire what we do today.

We first began serving consumers as Barclaycard, the credit card that rocked the UK over 50 years ago. Today, we partner with some of the most successful travel, entertainment, retail and affinity institutions in the U.S. 

 

Historical timeline

Here are a few highlights of how we got to where we are now:

1966

Barclays launches Barclaycard, the first credit card in the UK.

1967

Barclays introduces the world’s first ATM.

2004

Barclays acquires Juniper Bank, a credit card company formed in 2000, creating Barclaycard US.

2009

Barclaycard becomes a top-10 credit card issuer in the U.S.

2011

Barclaycard launches the first mobile app for cardmembers.

2012

Barclaycard introduces a new online bank with savings and CDs.

2016

Barclaycard launches a personal loan product online in the U.S.

2018

Barclaycard rebrands to Barclays in the U.S., offering a range of personal banking products to U.S. consumers.
