Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the credit offer details?

    Offer subject to credit approval. Not everyone will qualify for the Hawaiian Airlines Business Mastercard credit card. If at the time of your application you do not meet the credit criteria previously established for this offer, or the income you report is insufficient based on your obligations, we may not be able to open an account for you. This offer is available to new cardmembers only. Please see Terms and Conditions for more information about rates, fees, the annual fee, costs, and terms for this particular offer before applying.

  • How do I earn HawaiianMiles?

    Cardmembers will earn two (2) miles for every one U.S. dollar ($1.00) of eligible direct purchases less credits, returns and adjustments ("Net purchases") from Hawaiian Airlines made using their card. HA Vacation Packages and Duty Free purchases made in-flight on HAwaiian Airlines do not qualify as direct purchases from Hawaiian Airlines. Cardmembers will earn one (1) mile for each one U.S. dollar ($1.00) of all other Net Purchases. Signing Individual can earn up to ten (10) miles for each one dollar ($1.00) of eligible purchases made at participating partners; see Hawaiian Airlines website for details. There is no limit to the total miles the Signing Individual can earn as long as the program continues and the credit card account is open and in good standing. To redeem miles for flights on Hawaiian Airlines, Signing Individual should 'Sign-in' to their HawaiianMiles Account at HawaiianAirlines.com. All mileage credits and awards are subject to further terms and conditions described in the Hawaiian Airlines Business Mastercard Rewards Program Rules and the HawaiianMiles Program.

  • How many bonus miles will I earn for activating and using the card?

    Signing Individual will receive forty thousand (40,000) bonus miles after qualifying first purchase. This one-time bonus miles offer is valid for first-time cardmembers with new accounts only. Existing cardmembers and existing accounts are not eligible for this offer. This is a limited-time offer and may be canceled at any time without notice. Signing Individual should allow 4-6 weeks for bonus miles to be deposited in their HawaiianMiles account after the close of the billing statement after the qualifying purchase (that is not later returned or rescinded) has posted.

  • How do I get the 20,000 or 40,000 annual bonus miles?

    Signing Individuals earn twenty thousand (20,000) annual bonus miles on their Hawaiian Airlines Business Mastercard account anniversary when they spend between fifty thousand ($50,000) and ninety-nine thousand, nine hundred ninety-nine dollars ($99,999) in purchases made with the card acccount, less credits, returns, adjustments ("Net Purchases") within an anniversary year, or earn forty thousand (40,000) annual bonus miles on their account anniversary when they spend at least one hundred thousand ($100,000) in Net Purchases within an anniversary year. Please see the Hawaiian Airlines Business Mastercard Rewards Program Rules in the Terms and Conditions.

  • Is there a limit to the number of miles I can earn?

    No. There is no limit to the total number of miles that can be earned with your Hawaiian Airlines Business Mastercard as long as the program continues and your credit card account is open and in good standing.

  • How do I redeem my HawaiianMiles?

    To redeem miles for flights on Hawaiian Airlines, “Sign-in” to your HawaiianMiles account at HawaiianAirlines.com. Please visit HawaiianAirlines.com for more details about Hawaiian Airlines redemption policies and procedures.

  • Do my HawaiianMiles expire?

    Earned miles do not expire if there is HawaiianMiles account activity (earnings or redemptions). If there is no activity within 18 consecutive months, Hawaiian Airlines will expire the miles accrued on a member's HawaiianMiles account.

  • Are there any blackout dates for award travel?

    No. Fly with no blackout dates to the Hawaiian Islands, North America, International and South Pacific destinations.

  • What is the 50% off companion travel discount?

    Signing Individual is eligible to receive a one-time 50% off discount toward one published roundtrip coach fare for a Companion. One-time 50% off discount is valid for 13 months from account open date. Allow up to 5 days after account approval for companion discount to be available for use. Discount is valid only for coach fare for named HawaiianMiles member's companion for roundtrip travel between North America and Hawaii booked directly through the Signing Individual’s HawaiianMiles account using his or her Hawaiian Airlines Business Mastercard, and is subject to change. Named HawaiianMiles member and their companion must be booked on the same reservation; discount will be applied to the companion coach fare. Ticketing by E ticket only. Discount not applied to taxes, fees, and surcharges. Government taxes and fees and carrier-imposed fees will be applied to each ticket purchase. Other restrictions apply. Cost subject to change. Fares are subject to availability. Additional baggage charges may apply. Offer may not be combined with, applied to, or applied towards HawaiianMiles flight awards redeemed using miles. Offer not valid on Neighbor Island flights, multi-city reservations, code share flights with Hawaiian codeshare airlines, First Class or Extra Comfort fares, or open tickets. Discount cannot be applied to tickets already purchased. eCertificate offer can only be redeemed through HawaiianMiles' member's account on HawaiianAirlines.com. eCertificates are not transferable, have no cash value, and will not be replaced if lost or stolen. eCertificate may not be combined with, applied to, or used in conjunction with any other discount, promotional offer or tour package. eCertificate does not apply to Vacation Package bookings, including flights booked with a hotel on HawaiianAirlines.com. Flights booked in H and K class of service may not qualify for other airline frequent flyer miles. Standard Hawaiian Airlines eCertificate Terms and Conditions apply. See HawaiianAirlines.com for complete terms. 

  • What does no foreign transaction fees mean?

    No foreign transaction fees means you don't pay additional fees on purchases you make while traveling in another country.

  • What is the Share Miles benefit?

    Signing Individuals can receive HawaiianMiles from friends and family online at HawaiianAirlines.com. You must have a current email address on file within your HawaiianMiles account to receive HawaiianMiles transferred with the Share Miles program. You may receive HawaiianMiles transferred from friends and family up to a maximum of ten (10) times per calendar year. The HawaiianMiles transferer must be a current HawaiianMiles account holder and may perform an unlimited amount of transfer transactions using the Share Miles program. There are no transaction fees for HawaiianMiles transfers, no charges per mile, and no minimum or maximum number of HawaiianMiles transferred to you.

  • How do I receive discounted award flights?

    You are eligible to receive a discount on flight awards for travel on Hawaiian Airlines to select destinations. Discounted flight awards are accessible through your HawaiianMiles account online at HawaiianAirlines.com.

  • Is there a cost to get additional cards for employees?

    No. There is no additional annual fee for employee cards.

  • What is a chip card?

    In addition to storing your account information on a magnetic stripe on the back of your credit card, chip cards also keep your account information on a small electronic circuit (a "chip") located inside your card. Since the chip uses encryption technology, your account information is very secure. Most important, chip-technology credit cards are the standard for making transactions in many parts of the world. The Hawaiian Airlines Business Mastercard is a chip-and-signature card with PIN capability for use at unattended terminals, offering US cardmembers maximum acceptance and convenience when traveling abroad.
