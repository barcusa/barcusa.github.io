Rewards Summary

    Earning Diamond Plus Points™: Cardmember(s) earn "Diamond Plus Points™" in the following amounts based upon the type of purchase transaction charged to the Account:

    a) Earn two (2) Diamond Plus Points™ or each one dollar ($1) in net purchases made on Diamond Resorts International® purchases

    b) Earn one (1) Diamond Plus Point™ for each one dollar ($1) spent on all other purchases

    The following transactions are excluded from purchases and do not earn Diamond Plus Points™ unless stated: Diamond Resorts International® timeshare down payments, interest, finance charges, balance transfers, convenience checks, cash advances and credit fees. Restrictions apply. There is a fee for Balance Transfers. Balance Transfer Checks and Convenience Checks do not qualify for balance transfer points. Please see the Terms and Conditions for more information.

    First Use Bonus Diamond Plus Points™: Receive 2,500 Bonus Diamond Plus Points™ in addition to the regular Diamond Plus Points™ you earn after making an initial retail purchase with this card. Bonus Diamond Plus Points™ will post within 60 days of your first purchase. This promotion is limited to new cardmembers.

    Earn 500 bonus Diamond Plus Points™ for every $2,000 in qualifying net purchases made using your Diamond Resorts International Mastercard, up to a maximum of 5,000 bonus points, for as long as you remain a cardmember. Timeshare down payments made at the time of account opening, and any timeshare down payments made subsequently on other Diamond Resorts International properties, will not count as qualified spend toward your 500 bonus Diamond Plus Points™.

    Diamond Plus Points™ can be redeemed for statement credits toward Diamond Resorts International® purchases, airfare purchases, and car rental purchases provided those purchases are $50 or more and made within the past 120 days. Airfare and car rental purchases deemed eligible for redemption are defined by the merchant category code. Purchases made at merchants that do not process transactions under these terms or that use incorrect merchant category codes will not qualify. Barclays is not responsible for incorrectly coded purchases.
