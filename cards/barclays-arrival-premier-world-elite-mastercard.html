Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the Barclays Arrival® Premier World Elite Mastercard® credit offer details?

    This offer is subject to credit approval. Not everyone will qualify for the Barclays Arrival® Premier World Elite Mastercard®. If, at the time of your application, you do not meet the credit criteria previously established for this offer, or the income you report is insufficient based on your obligations, we may not be able to open an account for you. This offer is available to new cardmembers only. Please see Terms and Conditions for more information about rates, fees, the annual fee, costs and terms for this offer before applying.

     

    Annual Fee: $150. For purchases and balance transfers, the variable APR is 17.49%, 21.49%, or 24.49% depending upon our review of your application and your credit history at account opening. The variable APR for cash advances is 26.74%. The APRs on your account will vary with the market based on the Prime Rate. The minimum monthly interest charge will be $0.50. Balance transfer fee: 3% (min. $5). Cash advance and cash equivalent fees: 5% (min. $10). Foreign transaction fee: $0. The APRs on your account will vary with the market based on the Prime Rate and are subject to change. For more information see the “About the Variable APRs on Your Account” section of the Terms and Conditions. See Terms and Conditions for updated and more information about the terms of this offer. 

     

    About This Offer: You must be at least 18 years of age. This offer is available only to applicants who are residents of the United States, with the exception of Iowa and the US territories. You may not be eligible for this offer if you currently have or previously had an account with us in the Arrival, Arrival Plus and/or Arrival Premier programs. In addition, you may not be eligible for this offer if, at any time during our relationship with you as the issuer of an account in this program, we have cause, as determined by us in our sole discretion, to suspect that the account is being obtained or will be used for abusive or gaming activity (such as, but not limited to, obtaining or using the account to maximize rewards earned in a manner that is not consistent with typical consumer activity and/or multiple credit card account applications/openings). If at the time of your application you do not meet the credit or income criteria previously established for this offer, or the income you report is insufficient based on your current obligations, we will not be able to open an account for you. Please review the materials provided with your card for details.

  • What are loyalty bonus miles?

    Loyalty bonus miles earned in addition to the unlimited 2X miles you earn on every purchase. You can earn loyalty bonuses up to 25,000 miles each year. Spend $15,000 on purchases, get 15,000 miles. Spend an additional $10,000 on purchases, get an additional 10,000 miles, for a total of 25,000 miles after $25,000 spent on purchases. Loyalty bonus miles are earned in addition to unlimited 2X miles on every purchase. Please note that your total purchases include purchases by you and your authorized user(s) and that credits, returns and adjustments will be deducted from the total.

  • How do I earn miles?

    Use your Barclays Arrival Premier World Elite Mastercard for all your purchases and earn unlimited 2X miles on every purchase.  You can also earn loyalty bonus miles up to 25,000 miles during each year. In addition, please note that your total purchases include purchases by you and your authorized user(s) and that credits, returns and adjustments will be deducted from the total.

  • What can I redeem for?

    You can redeem your miles for travel and cash back statement credits, gift cards and merchandise.  Redeem your miles for travel statement credits, starting at 10,000 miles for $100 toward all or a portion of your qualifying travel purchase of $100 or more made within the last 120 days. Redeem your miles for cash back statement credits starting at 5,000 miles for $25. Redeem your miles for a variety of gift cards from national retailers to dining establishments starting at 5,000 miles for $25. Redeem your miles for a selection of merchandise tailored to travel and convenience. Merchandise redemption values vary based on what you are redeeming for. Redeeming miles for travel statement credits offers the best value.  

  • Which travel purchases are eligible for redemption?

    Travel purchases eligible for redemptions are defined as airlines, hotels, motels, timeshares, campgrounds, car rental agencies, cruise lines, travel agencies, discount travel sites, trains, buses, taxis, limousines, ferries and your account annual fee ("Travel Purchases"). Please note, some merchants that provide transportation and travel-related services are not included in this category. For example, real estate agents, websites or owners that rent properties, in-flight goods and services, merchants within airports, and merchants that rent trailers, trucks, and other vehicles for the purpose of hauling. To qualify for redemption, Travel Purchases must be submitted by merchants using the eligible merchant category codes for the above categories. Barclays is not responsible for incorrectly coded Travel Purchases. 

  • Is there a limit to the amount of miles I can earn?

    No, there is no limit to the amount of 2X miles you can earn for every $1 you spend on purchases less credits, returns and adjustments. There is a maximum to the number of loyalty bonus miles that can be earned each year. You can earn a maximum of 25,000 loyalty bonus miles after spending $25,000 in Net Purchases each year.

  • Will my miles expire?

    No, as long as the program continues and the account is open, active and in good standing, your miles will not expire.

  • How does the Global Entry benefit work?

    Global Entry Membership allows international travelers expedited entry upon arrival in the United States. If approved, Global Entry members can use automated kiosks in most major U.S. airports, rather than having to go through the traditional arrival process.  Primary cardmembers will receive one statement credit for the $100 application fee every five years, once the fee is charged to the account.  Please allow 2-3 weeks for the credit to appear on your statement. The ability to receive a $100 application fee credit will reset once every 5 years. 

  • How does Mastercard® Airport Experiences provided by Lounge Key work?

    Cardmembers can use their Barclays Arrival® Premier World Elite Mastercard®, and the program’s supporting website, airport.mastercard.com, and app seamlessly, to access a variety of benefits. You’ll have access, for a fee, to over 850 lounges, in over 400 airports worldwide (120+ countries), regardless of their airline, frequent flyer membership, or class of ticket. In addition, there are offers from participating merchants within the airport via website or app. Before traveling, cardholders can log on to airport.mastercard.com or access the mobile app (search for “Mastercard Airport Experiences” in the app store) to view a list of participating lounges.  

  • Can you explain the miles transfer program?

    You can transfer your miles to participating travel loyalty programs. Miles required for miles transfer redemptions vary, depending on what you are redeeming for. The selection of miles transfer partners are subject to availability and the number of miles needed to redeem them may change at any time. You may only transfer miles if you are a primary cardmember, and only to a participating travel loyalty program account belonging to you.

  • What is a chip card?

    Your chip card offers a more secure, convenient way to make purchases in the U.S. and abroad. When used at chip-card terminals, it uses encryption technology to protect your account information, reduce the risk of fraud, and make your card difficult to counterfeit. Chip cards are already standard in more than 130 countries, and U.S. merchants are quickly making the switch to chip. So you can feel confident that your card will be accepted nearly everywhere - at registers that use magnetic stripes, at chip-card terminals, and even at self-service terminals that require a PIN. But whether you pay using chip technology, or swipe your card, you'll be covered by our $0 Fraud Liability protection for any unauthorized transactions you report to us. 

  • What is $0 Fraud Liability protection?

    $0 Fraud Liability protection means you're not responsible for unauthorized charges you report to us.

  • What does no foreign transaction fees mean?

    No foreign transaction fees means you don’t pay additional transaction fees on purchases made while traveling abroad.
