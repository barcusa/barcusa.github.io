Rewards Summary

    Bonus Miles: Applicants who apply and are approved for the World Mastercard with $69 Annual Fee will receive forty thousand (40,000) bonus miles after using their new account for $500 or more in net retail purchases (that are not later returned or rescinded) within ninety (90) days of account opening and payment of the annual fee. Bonus miles will be sent to Frontier at the close of your billing statement after qualifying transaction(s) have been posted. Bonus miles are contingent upon payment of the applicable annual fee (bonus miles awards may be forfeited if the applicable annual fee is later waived or rescinded).

    Earning Miles: Applicants who apply and are approved for the World Mastercard with $69 Annual Fee will receive two (2) miles per one dollar ($1) spent on FlyFrontier.com purchases and one (1) mile per one dollar ($1) spent on all other purchases.

    Award Redemption Fee Waiver: Award redemption fees assessed by Frontier Airlines will be waived for the primary cardmember when award tickets are reserved using miles from the cardmember's Frontier Airlines EarlyReturns account, and all applicable fees/taxes (starting from $5.60 for a one-way ticket) are paid using the Frontier Airlines Mastercard at the time of award booking. Frontier Airlines is solely responsible for waiving the award redemption fee. Please visit Flyfrontier.com for full details on award redemption fees.

    Award Travel: Roundtrip award travel may be booked once a minimum of twenty thousand (20,000) miles have been earned and deposited into member's EarlyReturns® account. Award tickets on Frontier flights are subject to availability and capacity controls. Subject to taxes and fees starting from $11.20. Please see important information about the Frontier Airlines EarlyReturns program in the Terms and Conditions.

    $100 Frontier Airlines Flight Discount Voucher Cardmembers with the $69 annual fee card earn a $100 Frontier Airlines Flight Discount Voucher after your account anniversary when you have spent $2,500 or more in purchases on your account during your cardmembership year (each 12 month period and including your account anniversary date). The Flight Discount Voucher expires 180 days from date of issue. the Flight Discount Voucher will be issued approximately 6-8 weeks after the account anniversary date if the $2,500 in purchases requirement was met during the cardmembership year. The Flight Discount Voucher will be delivered via email to the primary cardmember's email address on file with Barclaycard and will also be available by logging into the primary cardmember's online account at barclaycardus.com. Travel must be booked before the expiration date; however, the flight may be after the expiration date. All travel is valid only on Frontier Airlines flights operated by Frontier Airlines and does not qualify for frequent flyer mileage. The flight discount is only redeemable online at Flyfrontier.com. the voucher has no cash value and may not be sold, bartered, or traded. Additional term and conditions apply.

    FICO® Score Access: FICO is a registered trademark of Fair Isaac Corporation in the United States and other countries. Barclaycard offers FICO® Score access at its sole discretion. Not all accounts will have FICO® Score access. FICO® Score access is not a permanent feature of your account and may be removed at any time. To view your FICO® Score, your account with us must be open, active (having activity within the past 150 days) and in good standing.

    Your application for a Frontier Airlines Mastercard is your consent to Frontier Airlines and Barclays Bank Delaware (Barclaycard) to share information as required to administer the Frontier Airlines EarlyReturns® Mileage Program to earn and redeem miles.
