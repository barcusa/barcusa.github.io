Rewards Summary

    15,000 Wyndham Rewards bonus points are enough to redeem for one go freeSM award (free night) night. A go free award is good for 1 standard room at a participating Wyndham Rewards hotel up to room's maximum occupancy, subject to availability. Most hotels: applies to room rate only, including taxes. Alternatively, points can be redeemed for different Wyndham Rewards redemption options. Refer to Wyndhamrewards.com for all available reward redemption values and WyndhamRewards.com/terms for the Wyndham Reward’s Program full terms and conditions.

    First Purchase Bonus: Signature and Platinum Visa cardmembers will receive 15,000 bonus points after the first use of your account for a Purchase or Balance Transfer (that are not later returned or rescinded).  Please allow 8-12 weeks after the applicable qualifying transaction for Wyndham Rewards to receive notice and post bonus points to your membership account.  There is a fee for Balance Transfers. Balance Transfer Checks and Convenience Checks do not qualify for bonus points. See the Card Terms and Conditions for complete details about this offer.

    Earning Points: Signature and Platinum Visa No Annual Fee Cardmembers will earn three (3) Points for every one dollar ($1.00) spent on eligible new Net Purchase transactions made with the Account on participating hotel purchases, Wyndham Vacation Rental North America properties, and on-property spend and maintenance fees (excluding timeshare down payment transactions) at Club Wyndham, WorldMark by Wyndham and Shell Vacations Club timeshare properties; two (2) points per one dollar ($1) spent on eligible gas, utility and grocery store (excluding Target and Walmart) purchases; and one (1) point per one dollar ($1) spent on all other purchases (except Wyndham Vacation Ownership timeshare downpayment transactions at Club Wyndham, WorldMark by Wyndham and Shell Vacations Club properties, which do not earn points).

    Gas, utility, and grocery store (excluding Target and Walmart) Net Purchase transactions made with the Account are eligible to earn 2 points per $1 spent only to the extent they are submitted to Barclays by the applicable merchant using the merchant category code corresponding to the gas, utility or grocery store category (as applicable). Neither Wyndham Hotel Group, LLC nor Barclays is responsible for incorrectly coded transactions.

    Visit wyndhamrewards.com/terms or call Wyndham Rewards Member Services for Participating Hotel Chains and participating Wyndham Rewards hotels. Participating Hotel Chains and participating Wyndham Rewards hotels are subject to change at any time without notice.

    For more information on Wyndham Vacation Rentals North America, including an inventory of properties, visit wyndhamvacationrentals.com.

    For more information on Wyndham Vacation Ownership, including an overview of resorts, please visit www.wyndhamworldwide.com/category/wyndham-vacation-ownership.

    Wyndham Rewards Member Level: Signature and Platinum Visa Primary No Annual Fee Cardmembers will receive Wyndham Rewards Gold Level.  Subject to the Wyndham Rewards Terms & Conditions, such primary cardmember will continue as a Wyndham Rewards Gold Level Member as long as their Wyndham Rewards Visa account is open and in good standing.  Please allow 2-8 weeks after account opening for this update to be made to your Wyndham Rewards Member Level. Member Levels is a part of the Wyndham Rewards program. For more information about Member Levels, including the perks available to each Member Level, visit www.wyndhamrewards.com/levels.
