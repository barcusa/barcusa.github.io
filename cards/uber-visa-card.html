Rewards Summary

    Bonus Points: The primary cardmember will earn 10,000 bonus points, redeemable for $100 in Uber credits, statement credits or gift cards after spending $500 on net purchases on your Uber Visa Card within the first ninety (90) days from the account open date. This one-time bonus points offer is valid for first-time cardmembers with new accounts only. Existing Uber Visa cardmembers are not eligible for this offer. The bonus points will be awarded after qualifying net purchases have posted to your account.

    Earning Points: Cardmembers will earn a percentage of purchase amount ("% back"), which will be awarded as points. Cardmembers earn 4% back/4 points for every $1.00 spent on net purchases made through UberEATS and at restaurants, as defined by merchant category codes, which includes restaurants, fast food restaurants and bars. Cardmembers earn 3% back/3 points for every $1.00 spent on net purchases on airfare, hotels and vacation home rentals (including travel agencies and home share exchanges), as defined by merchant category codes. Cardmembers earn 2% back/2 points for every $1.00 spent on eligible digital net purchases which includes purchases that occur online or in a mobile device but not utilities or contracted services. Cardmembers also earn 2% back/2 points on video and music streaming services and Uber. Cardmembers earn 1% back/1 point for every $1.00 spent on net purchases everywhere else. Barclays is not responsible for the category codes that merchants use. There is no limit to the total points cardmembers can earn as long as the program continues and the account is open and in good standing. Balance transfers, cash advances, cash equivalent transactions, fees, finance charges and unauthorized/fraudulent transactions do not earn points.

    $50 Credit: You may earn up to a $50 credit for net purchases from eligible digital music, video and shopping subscription services after you spend $5,000 or more in total net purchases on your account during each 12-month period through and including your account anniversary date. When you meet the $5,000 spend threshold, you will receive statement credits toward each new eligible digital subscription service transaction when it posts to your account.

    Mobile Phone Protection: Enjoy up to $600 in supplemental insurance coverage against theft of, damage to or involuntary and accidental parting of your cell phone not otherwise covered by another insurance policy when you make your total monthly cell phone bill payment with your Uber Visa Card. See your Guide to Benefits on your online account for complete details about coverage qualifications and replacement or repair reimbursement.

    Exclusive Access: Cardmembers will be selected to take advantage of exclusive access to select events and offers from time to time, primarily anticipated in cities such as New York, San Francisco, Los Angeles, Chicago, and D.C.

    In-app redemption: Points can be redeemed for Uber credits directly in the Uber app. Points can also be redeemed for cash back or gift cards. All redemptions are at a 1% value.
